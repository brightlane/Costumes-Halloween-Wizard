Group costumes are one of the best parts of Halloween — when everyone shows up coordinated, it's always the most memorable look of the night. Here are the best group Halloween costume ideas for 2026.

Group Costume Ideas by Size

Groups of 2 (couples) — See our full couples costumes guide for duo-specific ideas.

Groups of 3–4 — Try TV show casts, movie ensembles, or themed color palettes (e.g. Teletubbies, Ninja Turtles, Powerpuff Girls). Browse TV show costumes for ready-made groups.

Groups of 5+ — Large groups work best with a clear theme: decades (70s, 80s, 90s), card suits, or the seven deadly sins. See decades costumes for retro group ideas.

Family groups — Parents and kids together? Check matching family costumes for coordinated sets across all ages.

Pro Tips for Group Costumes

Order everyone's costumes at the same time to guarantee consistent sizing and delivery. Designate one person to coordinate — group orders can ship together for easy tracking.
